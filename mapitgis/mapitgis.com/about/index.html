About Mapit GIS

Built in the field.
For the field.

From a solo developer measuring land with a phone in 2013 to a cross-platform GIS ecosystem trusted by professionals worldwide. This is our story.

What we believe

Offline-first

Every version of every app works without a data connection. Cloud features are additive, never required.

Open standards

GeoPackage, WFS, WMTS, KML - we choose open formats over proprietary lock-in. Your data is yours.

Field-tested

Features come from real feedback. The barcode scanner exists because an arborist needed it. PostgreSQL export because a utilities company asked.

No compromises

Native apps on every platform. Swift on iOS, Kotlin on Android, Go on the server. No cross-platform shortcuts.

Our journey

Four product generations. One mission: professional GIS that works where you work.

The Beginning

Open Standards

The Unification

What’s next

Mapit GIS is becoming a full platform - from field collection to cloud collaboration to desktop analysis.

Built-in NTRIP Client

Centimetre-accuracy RTK positioning integrated directly into the app on both Android and iOS. No companion app required.

Mapit GIS Cloud

Web platform for project sync, team collaboration, and managing GeoPackage data from any browser.

QGIS Plugin

Seamless desktop-to-field workflows. Prepare projects in QGIS, push them to Mapit GIS in the field, and sync changes back.

High-Accuracy Transforms

Sub-decimetre coordinate transformations via our transform platform, supporting OSTN15, RDNAPTRANS, and custom grids.
