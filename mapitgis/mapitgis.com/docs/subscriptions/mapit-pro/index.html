Mapit Pro

Mapit Pro unlocks the full power of Mapit GIS on your mobile device. It removes all Free-tier limits and gives you access to every core feature.

note

On iOS, external GNSS receiver connections are currently limited to TCP/IP only. On Android, both Bluetooth and TCP/IP are supported.

Free tier limits​

The Free tier is fully functional for small projects and evaluation:

  • 1 project - enough to try out the app
  • 3 layers per project
  • 20 features per layer
  • Limited import/export - basic formats only

Upgrading to Pro removes all of these limits instantly.

How to subscribe​

Mapit Pro is available as an annual subscription through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).

  1. Open Mapit GIS
  2. Go to Settings → Subscription
  3. Tap Subscribe to Pro

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time from your device's subscription settings.

Restoring purchases​

If you reinstall the app or switch devices, your subscription is automatically restored when you sign in with the same Apple ID or Google account.
