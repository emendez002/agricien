We use both session and persistent Cookies for the purposes set out below:

  • Necessary / Essential Cookies - Type: Session Cookies. Administered by: Us. Purpose: These Cookies are essential to provide You with services available through the Website and to enable You to use some of its features. They help to authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. Purpose: These Cookies allow us to remember choices You make when You use the Website, such as remembering your login details or language preference. The purpose of these Cookies is to provide You with a more personal experience and to avoid You having to re-enter your preferences every time You use the Website.
  • Tracking and Performance Cookies - Type: Persistent Cookies. Administered by: Third-Parties. Purpose: These Cookies are used to track information about traffic to the Website and how users use the Website. The information gathered via these Cookies may directly or indirectly identify you as an individual visitor. This is because the information collected is typically linked to a pseudonymous identifier associated with the device you use to access the Website. We may also use these Cookies to test new advertisements, pages, features or new functionality of the Website to see how our users react to them.

For more information about the cookies we use and your choices regarding cookies, please visit our Cookies Policy.

Scope​

This section covers privacy practices specific to the Mapit GIS iOS application.

Application:

  • Mapit GIS, available on the Apple App Store

Data Collection​

The Mapit GIS iOS application is designed with an offline-first approach. All survey data, geographic features, and attachments are stored locally on your device. The application does not collect analytics, does not serve advertisements, and does not transmit your survey data to any external servers.

The application does not currently require user authentication or account creation.

Purchases​

If you purchase a subscription from the Apple App Store, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple. We have access to subscription status information provided by Apple to verify your entitlements. Please see Apple's Privacy Policy for further details on how Apple handles purchase data.

Third-Party Services​

The iOS application uses the following third-party services:

Mapbox Maps SDK​

The application uses the Mapbox Maps SDK and Mapbox Search SDK to provide map display, geocoding, and place search functionality. When using map and search features, the application communicates with Mapbox servers. Mapbox may collect certain data automatically, including:

  • Telemetry data: Anonymised usage data about map interactions to improve Mapbox maps and services.
  • Location data: General location information when the map is in use, used to improve map accuracy and services. When using the search feature, your approximate location may be sent to Mapbox to provide location-relevant results.
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, and screen resolution.

For more information, please see the Mapbox Privacy Policy.

NGA GeoPackage Libraries​

The application uses open-source GeoPackage and projection libraries developed by the National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA). These libraries operate entirely locally on your device and do not transmit any data to external servers.

Permissions​

The iOS application may request the following permissions:

  • Location Services - The application's primary purpose is to carry out precise GIS measurements related to location. The application cannot function without this permission. Both precise and approximate location may be used. The application may also use background location to continue tracking your position whilst the app is not in the foreground.
  • Camera - Required to carry out certain tasks during surveys, such as recording photographic evidence related to collected features or scanning QR and bar codes within forms.
  • Local Network - Required to connect to external GNSS receivers (such as Trimble devices) via TCP/IP for high-accuracy positioning.

Data Stored on Your Device​

The application stores the following data locally on your device:

  • GeoPackage databases containing your geographic features, layers, and attributes
  • Attachments (photographs, videos, audio) associated with collected features
  • Application settings such as your preferred basemap, coordinate format, and units
  • A device identifier stored securely in your device's Keychain, used to identify the device for potential future cloud synchronisation features

You can access and manage your data files through the iOS Files app. No data is automatically uploaded to any cloud service.

What We Do Not Do​

The Mapit GIS iOS application:

  • Does not contain any advertising or ad-tracking SDKs
  • Does not collect analytics or usage telemetry (beyond what Mapbox may collect as described above)
  • Does not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Does not upload your survey data to any external servers
